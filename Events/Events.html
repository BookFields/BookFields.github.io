Meet Karamo & Jason Brown at Barnes & Noble The Grove!

Join us on Sunday, November 3rd at 11:00AM to celebrate the release of I Am Perfectly Designed with Queer Eye’s Karamo Brown and his son, Jason Brown. Co-authors Karamo and Jason will read from the book, and then both Karamo and Jason will sign their title after answering fan questions. Customers are welcome to take photos with the authors. Photos will be taken by a bookseller or parent/guardian. Karamo and Jason will only sign and personalize the featured title.



Eva Chen at Barnes & Noble The Grove

Join us on Saturday, November 2nd at 11:00AM to celebrate the release of Juno Valentine and the Fantastic Fashion Adventure with author Eva Chen and illustrator Derek Desierto. Eva will read from the book, and then both Eva and Derek will sign their title. Customers are welcome to take photos with the author and illustrator. Photos will be taken by a bookseller or parent/guardian.



Books, Gourmet Food, and Library Love at the Queen Mary

What’s better than a Day of the Dead book event that offers gourmet dining, and takes place on an 85-year-old, Art Deco, haunted ship? How about one that offers all that, and raises money for a new library? This fall, the RMS Queen Mary will debut a spectacular event for literature and library lovers, history buffs, ship aficionados, and gourmet foodies, at the Queen Mary Secret Spice Cafe Dinner and Tour, on Saturday, November 2, 2019 in Long Beach, California.

There are two types of tickets for this event. Purchase of the General Admission ticket entitles the ticket holder entry into the signing line, as well as one signed copy of I Am Perfectly Deisgned. Purchase of the Youth Ticket with Companion allows for a parent/guardian to attend with their child.
